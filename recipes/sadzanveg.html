Sadza nema Veggie

Description

Sadza nema veggie is a Zimbabwean delicacy that is filling and tasty. Sadza is a local favorite made from mealie-meal and water. The meal is mainly served with a side of vegetables and meat (you cannot go wrong with beef stew).

Ingredients

  • Mealie Meal
  • Beef
  • Spinach (or leafy green of choice)

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup (150ml) of mealie meal in a mediumsized pot
  2. Add 4 cups of warm water to the pot
  3. Bring to a boil while stirring
